Meet The Team

Brittany

Jr Software Developer

Brittany is very passionate about dog adoption and proper pet care. When she was in the Air Force she made it a point to regularly visit the military working dogs and care for them when possible. The working life span for a MWD is 10 years. Aftwards they are lucky to go to loving homes that understand their previous duties and protective nature. Luckly programs now exist for the former handlers to adopt them after the dogs are shipped back to Texas. These dogs are labeled as equiptment but to so many of us that served, they are our battle buddies.
