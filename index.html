Welcome To Rexcue!

Welcome to Rexcue!

You have decided that it’s time to add a pet to your family. After some consideration, you’ve fallen in love with a particular dog breed. Many people then look for a dog breeder to make their purebred-pup dream come true.

Like any responsible future pet parent, you still have some questions before you take the plunge and choose your new dog, which may be why you’re here! Did you know that there are LOTS of purebred dogs waiting for the perfect forever home at shelters and rescue groups? Not only that, but there are also special breed-rescue groups who dedicate their efforts to finding homes for just one breed of dog.

Dogs of every type and breed can end up in shelters. Before you start checking for a breeder to find your next family pet, we recommend starting your search at your local shelter where lots of good boys and good girls are anxiously waiting to show you love!

Take our Personality Quiz!

Are you unsure on what which breed of dog you would like to adopt? No worries! Our team here at Rexcue has you covered with our highly comprehensive personality quiz! After answering a few short questions on your own personality, we link you with a specific breed of dogs that would be a perfect match for you and your family!
